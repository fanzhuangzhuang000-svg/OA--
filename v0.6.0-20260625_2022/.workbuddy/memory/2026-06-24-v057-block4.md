# V0.5.7 块 4 完工 — 2026-06-24 20:00

## 交付内容

### 后端
1. **service `RepairCostStat`** (`app/Services/`, 270 行)
   - `overview()`: 7 KPI (单数/总成本/免费/付费/工时/已付/未付)
   - `byMonth($n)`: 近 N 月月度成本 (默认 12, 1-36 限制)
   - `byProject()`: 按 project_id 汇总 (含 project_no)
   - `byCustomer()`: 按 customer_id 汇总 (含 paid/warranty 拆分)
   - `byMethod()`: 按 4 选 1 维修方式 (含占比 %)
   - `thisMonth()`: 本月 + 合同金额 + 占比 (dashboard 用)
   - 状态过滤: `completed/closed/shipped_back` (计入成本)
   - 时间/客户/项目/方式/质保 5 维可过滤

2. **controller `RepairCostSummaryController`** (5 端点, 92 行)
   - `GET /api/repair-cost/overview`
   - `GET /api/repair-cost/by-month?months=N&from=&to=&customer_id=&project_id=&method_type=`
   - `GET /api/repair-cost/by-project`
   - `GET /api/repair-cost/by-customer`
   - `GET /api/repair-cost/by-method`
   - 全部要 token, query 参数透传到 service

3. **Dashboard 扩展** `DashboardController::maintenanceStats()`
   - cost 段加 4 字段: `this_month_total/this_month_warranty/this_month_paid/total_contract/cost_ratio_pct`
   - 旧 `monthly` 字段保留 (向后兼容)
   - `cost_ratio_pct` = 售后成本 / 合同金额 (0-100)

4. **routes** — 5 路由加在 `auth:api` 中间件组
